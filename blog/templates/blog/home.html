CONSTRUCTION of our Political Risk Tools

We want to redefine the approach of measuring, customizing, monitoring and forecasting Political Risks and Uncertainties
Our approach is to measure political risk with the power of the population and not experts

Understand the creation of our Political Risk Tools

There are many ways of measuring political risks. In the past years some methods have established. Primarily there are expert-based, news-based and data-based political risk measurements. Since many of these political risk measures often rely on different parameters, like experts or newspaper authors, we wanted to create a different approach of measuring political risk. Therefore, our political risk index is search-based, which means that the data is collected via search requests made on one of the biggest search enginges Google™. That means our poltical risk index does not rely on the opinion of experts or news article writers, but only relies on the uncertainty of a portion of the population in a country.
