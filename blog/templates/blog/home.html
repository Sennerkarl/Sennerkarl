Construction of our Political Risk Tools

We want to redefine the approach of measuring, customizing, monitoring and forecasting Political Risks and Uncertainties
Our approach is to measure political risk with the power of the population instead of relying on subjective experts

Overview Of The New Approach

Our goal is to gain new insights on political risk. Our tools seek to understand the view of the population. Research has shown a strong link between political risk and our evaluations.

Modern measuring technique

By utilizing Google data in a new way we are able to create specific and up-to-date political risk evaluations. We use modern algorithms and key words to extract the relevant data points for each region. This method also enables industry or company specific adaptations. For more information download our white paper.

Validation of our methods

We validated our data based on scientific standards. Event studies and regression techniques were used to validate the data and test our main hypotheses. The outcome of our research all pointed to a strong link between our data and political risk in the evaluated countries.

Understand the Basics of our Political Risk Measurement

In the past years a few methods have established. Primarily there are expert-based, news-based and data-based political risk measurements. Since many of these political risk measures often rely on different parameters, like experts or newspaper authors, we wanted to find a different approach for measuring political risk. Our political risk index is search-based, which means that the data is collected via search requests of the global population on the most popular search engine Google™. That means our poltical risk index does not rely on the opinion of experts or journalists, but relies on the felt uncertainty of the population in a country.
