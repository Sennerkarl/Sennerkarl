How sure came to life

Everything has a beginning

During our studies at one of the most modern campuses in Europe the WU we were encountered with a variety of political risk tools. However, none of them were truly accessible. Thinking about this issue the idea arose to evaluate political risk in an entirely novel way. We wanted data which could grasp a wide variety of perspectives and information which is easily accessible to most. This lead to Google™. Since then we have been advancing or research and our approaches to enable a solution for political risk for small, middle and large businesses.
