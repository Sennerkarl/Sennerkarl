What are Political Risk?

Download White Paper

With the creation of our masterthesis the passion for political risk rose tremendously. Our mission was to create an index, which comes form the people and represents the peoples uncertainty about a countries political situation, regulation and sanctions. All of these we wanted to create with a newly approached tool, which all of us are using every day: GOOGLE.™ With the data provision of Google Trends™ this became reality. We are in the beginning of our development phase and want to provide a solution for small, middle and big companies to monitor and forecast political risk in certain countries in many different time periods (yearly, monthly, daily and even hourly) at the expanse of very little costs. It was important for us to create a plitical risk tool, which can be used from everyone and can be adapted in any given way. For more information about our approach of measuring political risks download our white paper.
